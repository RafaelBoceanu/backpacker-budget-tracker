FAQ

Common questions

Is Bocora free to use?
Yes, completely free. There are no subscriptions, no premium tiers, and no paywalled features.
Do I need to create an account?
No account needed. Bocora stores all your data locally on your device, so there's nothing to sign up for and nothing stored on external servers.
Which currencies are supported?
Bocora supports over 150 currencies with automatic conversion using live exchange rates. If you're offline, it uses the most recently cached rates.
Can I use it on iPhone?
Yes. While the native app is currently Android-only, the web app at bocora.vercel.app works on any device including iPhone. You can add it to your home screen from Safari for an app-like experience.
What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?
Since data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling will remove your trips and expenses. We recommend exporting or noting down important trip data before uninstalling.
Does it work without internet?
Yes. Bocora is a Progressive Web App with full offline support. You can log expenses, view your budget, and use all features without a connection.
