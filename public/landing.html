FAQ

Common questions

Is Bocora free?
Yes, completely free. No subscriptions, no premium tiers, no paywalled features.
Do I need to create an account?
No account needed. Bocora stores everything locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.
Which currencies are supported?
Over 150 currencies with live conversion rates. When offline, Bocora uses the most recently cached rates so you're never stuck.
Does it work on iPhone?
Yes. The web app works on any device including iPhone. Open it in Safari and add it to your home screen for a native app feel. The Android app is available on Google Play.
Will I lose my data if I clear my browser?
Since data is stored locally, clearing your browser storage will remove your trips. We recommend noting down important totals before doing so.
Can I track multiple currencies in one trip?
Yes. Each expense can be logged in a different currency. Bocora converts all of them to your home currency for a unified total.
